# ROS 2 Assessment

## Overview

This assessment tests your understanding of ROS 2 fundamentals covered in the ROS 2 module. Complete this assessment after reviewing all ROS 2 content.

- Knowledge of nodes, topics, and services

Test your theoretical knowledge of ROS 2 concepts.
